Here are the specific, role-based tips (Tank, Healer, DPS) for every boss in the Season 1 Midnight Mythic+ dungeon pool, drawing directly from the provided class and dungeon guides.
Magisters' Terrace
Arcanotron Custos
🛡️ Tank: Beware of Repulsing Slam, which deals magical damage and knocks you back.
💉 Healer: Prepare for high incoming damage when the boss gains stacks of Unstable Energy or channels Refueling Protocol.
⚔️ DPS: Hold your major offensive cooldowns until the boss's energy fully depletes to 0, as this triggers a phase where it takes significantly increased damage.
Seranel Sunlash
🛡️ Tank: The boss buffs himself with Hastening Ward, increasing his attack speed and magic damage. Be ready with defensives, or have your group purge/shatter it (e.g., Wrecking Throw).
💉 Healer: Runic Mark requires heavy spot healing; coordinate with players so they don't step into Suppression Zones at the same time to clear it.
⚔️ DPS: You must step into a black Suppression Zone to avoid the Wave of Silence, but do not enter if an ally is currently inside clearing their Runic Mark.
Gemellus
🛡️ Tank: Keep the boss moving to stack him directly on top of his copies to maximize your group's cleave.
💉 Healer: Cosmic Sting and Astral Grasp cause high, random-target burst damage. Have spot-heals ready.
⚔️ DPS: All copies share a health pool due to Synaptic Nexus. Heavy AoE/cleave specs will excel here.
Degentrius
🛡️ Tank: Hulking Fragment is a massive tank buster that leaves a purple ticking zone. Use major defensives and immediately reposition the boss.
💉 Healer: Devouring Entropy applies a heavy DoT to the group roughly every 23 seconds.
⚔️ DPS: Assign quadrants before the pull. You must soak the Unstable Void Essences in your quadrant to prevent a group wipe.
Maisara Caverns
Muro'jin and Nekraxx
🛡️ Tank: Use active mitigation for Muro'jin's Flanking Spear (knocks back and bleeds). Keep both bosses stacked tightly.
💉 Healer: Infected Pinions applies a disease that increases damage taken. Coordinate dispels and use major healing CDs if this overlaps with Barrage.
⚔️ DPS: You must kill both bosses within 1-2 seconds of each other. If one dies early, the survivor gains a lethal stacking enrage. Bait Carrion Swoop into Freezing Traps.
Vordaza
🛡️ Tank: Drain Soul is a 4-second channel that deals heavy damage and absorbs your incoming healing. Use defensives to survive the channel.
💉 Healer: Withering Miasma deals constant group damage, but Necrotic Convergence is your ultimate throughput check. Prepare your biggest AoE cooldowns here.
⚔️ DPS: Do not attack the Unstable Phantoms. Instead, kite your fixated phantom into another player's phantom to make them collide and die.
Rak'tul, Vessel of Souls
🛡️ Tank: Spiritbreaker knocks you into the air and drops a Spectral Decay puddle. Move away from the group before the hit and position the boss near Soulbind Totems for cleave.
💉 Healer: Soulrending Roar applies the Withering Soul debuff to the whole group and shatters totems for heavy burst. Be proactive with your cooldowns.
⚔️ DPS: During the intermission, you must interrupt/CC the Malignant Souls to destroy them. This grants your group Spectral Residue, a massive stacking damage/healing buff.
Nexus-Point Xenas
Chief Corewright Kasreth
🛡️ Tank: Position the boss near Leyline Arrays so melee DPS targeted by Reflux Charge can maintain uptime while destroying the beams.
💉 Healer: Sparkburn applies to the whole party after Corespark Detonation. Have AoE healing ready to top the group up.
⚔️ DPS: Never touch a Leyline Array unless you are targeted by Reflux Charge, as they are otherwise lethal. Outrange Corespark Detonation.
Corewarden Nysarra
🛡️ Tank: Umbral Lash is a fast tank buster that leaves a 10-second debuff increasing your damage taken by 50% (Void Gash).
💉 Healer: Lightscar Flare deals continuous damage to players standing in the beam.
⚔️ DPS: Dodge the initial hit of Lightscar Flare (which disorients), then step into the beam to gain a 300% damage boost. Interrupt the Grand Nullifier adds immediately.
Lothraxion
🛡️ Tank: Save your defensives for the Searing Rend frontal cleave.
💉 Healer: Brilliant Dispersion puts a heavy DoT on players that requires major healing cooldowns.
⚔️ DPS: During the Divine Guile intermission, find and interrupt the clone with horns. Hunters can use Hunter's Mark to instantly reveal the real boss.
Windrunner Spire
Emberdawn
🛡️ Tank: Searing Beak deals physical damage and leaves a heavy 8-second fire DoT.
💉 Healer: Burning Gale is a heavy group damage channel; plan your strongest throughput cooldowns here.
⚔️ DPS: During the Burning Gale phase, stay close to the boss to minimize the distance you need to run to dodge the rotating fire-breath frontals.
Derelict Duo (Kalis and Latch)
🛡️ Tank: Bone Hack (Latch) is a heavy channel on you; use consistent mitigation.
💉 Healer: Curse of Darkness spawns fixating Dark Entities. Purge/dispel this immediately.
⚔️ DPS: Keep their health balanced to avoid the Broken Bond enrage. Maintain a strict interrupt rotation on Kalis's Shadow Bolt. Bait Latch's Heaving Yank into Kalis to stop her uninterruptible shriek.
Commander Kroluk
🛡️ Tank: Use defensives for the Rampage channel. Quickly pick up the Haunting Grunts during the intermission phases.
💉 Healer: Reckless Leap targets the farthest player, causing high spike damage and a DoT. Watch your ranged players carefully.
⚔️ DPS: You must stack within 5 yards of an ally to avoid being feared by Intimidating Shout. Focus the Phantasmal Mystic and Spectral Axethrower during the add phases.
The Restless Heart
🛡️ Tank: Tempest Slash knocks you back and increases physical damage taken. You can aim yourself to get knocked into a Turbulent Arrow to cancel the knockback and clear stacks.
💉 Healer: Monitor Squall Leap stacks on the group. If players don't clear them, the DoT damage quickly becomes unhealable.
⚔️ DPS: Step on Turbulent Arrows on the ground to clear your Squall Leap stacks, and use them to bounce over the expanding Bullseye Windblast ring.
Algeth'ar Academy
Vexamus
🛡️ Tank: Stand completely still and do not face the boss toward the group during Arcane Expulsion.
💉 Healer: Spot-heal players targeted by Mana Bomb.
⚔️ DPS: Soak Arcane Orbs before they reach the boss, but wait for your debuff to drop before soaking a second one.
Overgrown Ancient
🛡️ Tank: Barkbreaker makes you take 100% more physical damage. Be extremely careful if this overlaps with add spawns.
💉 Healer: Burst Forth requires AoE healing cooldowns, especially if the group isn't topped off.
⚔️ DPS: Stack tightly on the tank during Germinate to group up the Hungry Lashers for instant AoE burst. Interrupt the Ancient Branch's Healing Touch.
Crawth
🛡️ Tank: Savage Peck leaves a 10-second physical bleed.
💉 Healer: Deafening Screech applies heavy group damage; rotate cooldowns for each cast.
⚔️ DPS: Line up your offensive cooldowns when the boss is stunned by Firestorm for massive damage.
Echo of Doragosa
🛡️ Tank: Move the boss away from Arcane Rifts to give the group space to dodge the Uncontrolled Energy projectiles.
💉 Healer: Energy Bomb creates heavy spot healing pressure.
⚔️ DPS: Move to the edge of the room when you reach 2 stacks of Overwhelming Power so the resulting Arcane Rift drops safely out of the way.
Pit of Saron
Forgemaster Garfrost
🛡️ Tank: You must hit a Saronite Ore on the ground during Orebreaker or you will be stunned for 8 seconds.
💉 Healer: Siphoning Chill causes ramping group damage; Cryostomp requires immediate recovery healing.
⚔️ DPS: Break Line of Sight behind Saronite Ores when the boss casts Glacial Overload.
Ick and Krick
🛡️ Tank: Blight Smash leaves a toxic pool; reposition the boss immediately.
💉 Healer: Plague Expulsion and Shade Shift cause unavoidable, heavy group damage.
⚔️ DPS: Run away immediately when fixated by "Get 'Em, Ick!" and maintain kicks on Death Bolt. The bosses share health, so cleave heavily.
Scourgelord Tyrannus
🛡️ Tank: Scourgelord's Brand knocks you back and increases shadow damage taken by 200%, followed immediately by a jump (Reckoning). Save major CDs for this combo.
💉 Healer: Festering Pulse deals heavy damage while the Plaguespreader add is alive.
⚔️ DPS: Focus the Scourge Plaguespreader immediately when Army of the Dead is cast, and interrupt its Plague Bolt.
Seat of the Triumvirate
Zuraal the Ascended
🛡️ Tank: Void Slash is a heavy 3-hit combo (2 physical hits, 1 shadow hit).
💉 Healer: Oozing Slam occurs roughly every 55 seconds and requires focused spot healing and defensives.
⚔️ DPS: Slow, root, or CC the Coalesced Void add before it can reach the boss.
Saprish
🛡️ Tank: Keep the boss and all pets stacked as tightly as possible for cleave.
💉 Healer: Overload deals massive group damage; prepare your CDs (especially if players soaked Void Orbs right before).
⚔️ DPS: Interrupt Dread Screech from the Shadewing add immediately (it is lethal if missed). Stack up to handle Void Bombs easily.
Viceroy Nezhar
🛡️ Tank: Interrupt or spell-reflect Mind Blast.
💉 Healer: Void Storm and Mass Void Infusion put heavy AoE pressure on the group.
⚔️ DPS: Quickly swap to and kill Umbral Tentacles when they spawn. Watch the boss's energy to pre-position for the Collapsing Void knockback.
L'ura
🛡️ Tank: Keep track of how many rifts are opened by Abyssal Lance to anticipate incoming tank damage.
💉 Healer: Siphon Void is your major healing check window.
⚔️ DPS: The boss takes massively increased damage during Siphon Void. Align Bloodlust and your 2-3 minute cooldowns for these windows.
Skyreach
Ranjit
🛡️ Tank: Position carefully to avoid getting knocked off the platform during Gale Surge.
💉 Healer: Fan of Blades puts a heavy physical bleed on random players.
⚔️ DPS: Use personal defensives for the Fan of Blades bleed. Do not stand on the very edge of the platform to avoid knock-offs.
Araknath
🛡️ Tank: Watch the boss's arms; he will cast Fiery Smash directly in front of the raised arm. Step to the side to dodge!
💉 Healer: Supernova deals massive AoE damage. Use CDs.
⚔️ DPS: You must stay in melee range at all times, or the boss will cast lethal group-wide damage. Ensure 3-4 players are rotating to soak the Light Rays.
Rukhran
🛡️ Tank: Burning Claws is a physical hit plus an 8s fire DoT.
💉 Healer: Sunbreak deals significant group damage.
⚔️ DPS: Stay in melee range to prevent group-wide lethal damage. Focus down the Sunwing adds immediately, and hide behind pillars for Searing Quills.
High Sage Viryx
🛡️ Tank: Keep the boss near the Solar Zealot add so DPS can efficiently cleave them both.
💉 Healer: Scorching Ray targets a player with heavy, sustained spot damage.
⚔️ DPS: Interrupt Solar Blast. Slow, stun, or kill the Solar Zealot before it drops the targeted player off the platform (or use movement abilities/immunities to survive the drop).
